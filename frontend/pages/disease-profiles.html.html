Common Diseases

Malaria

Symptoms: Fever, chills, headache, nausea.

Treatment: Antimalarial medication such as artemisinin-based combination therapy (ACT).

Prevention: Use of insecticide-treated mosquito nets, indoor spraying with insecticides, antimalarial prophylaxis.

COVID-19

Symptoms: Fever, cough, shortness of breath, loss of taste or smell.

Treatment: Supportive care, antiviral medications, and in severe cases, mechanical ventilation.

Prevention: Vaccination, wearing masks, social distancing, frequent hand washing.

Learn More

Diabetes

Symptoms: Increased thirst, frequent urination, fatigue, blurred vision.

Treatment: Insulin therapy, oral medications, lifestyle changes (diet and exercise).

Prevention: Maintaining a healthy diet, regular physical activity, and managing weight.
