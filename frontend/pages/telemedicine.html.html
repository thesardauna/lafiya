Frequently Asked Questions

What is telemedicine?

Telemedicine allows you to connect with healthcare providers remotely using video conferencing technology for consultations, diagnoses, and treatment plans.

Is my consultation secure?

Yes, all consultations are conducted over a secure platform, ensuring the privacy and confidentiality of your health information.

How do I pay for my telemedicine appointment?

You can pay securely online using Google Pay, Paystack, or Flutterwave, ensuring a seamless and convenient payment experience.

Can I get a prescription through telemedicine?

Yes, your healthcare provider can issue prescriptions electronically, and you'll be able to access them via your patient portal.
